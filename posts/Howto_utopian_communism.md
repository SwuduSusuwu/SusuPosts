# Discussion
Global [communism](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJRjJrknMG4) is an almost-imposssible utopia with one huge world zone in which you are trusted to contribute what you can contribute, plus stores (with lines for customers) are replaced with distribution warehouses "staffed" with computer systems which give all you ask for (with trust that you do not ask for much more than what you must have to live on). Similar to animals in the forest (evolution encourages animals to support plant growth through spore dispersal plus removal of fallen branches, plus harvest just enough food from those plants to live on), except much more involved (humans would continue to use tools to reform the ground, plus sow crops with improved nutritional values, but for utopia most of the repetitive work is replaced with autonomous tools).

This is "almost impossible" since requires such tools that the amount you can trust everyone to contribute (without government) will do for all of us to live on. Are our [autonomous-tools](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PyxGis7Pl80) good enough to do so?

Our autonomous tools can [sow](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+sow+food+crops) \+ [harvest](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+harvest+food+crops) crops, [extract](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+extract+ores) \+ [smelt](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+smelt+ores+into+metals) mineral ores into metal, [mill metals into parts](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+mill+metal+parts), assemble parts [into automobiles](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+assemble+automobiles) (or [into houses](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=autonomous+tools+produce+modular+houses)), plus [produce more autonomous tools](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hLDbRm-98cs). But some human effort is still required for "edge cases" (such as tasks which our autonomous tools still do not have programs to do; problems which require humans to produce new solutions). Global communism requires sufficient trust in others to do such to improve the global pool / commune.
