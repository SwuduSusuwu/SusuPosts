# Intro
The solution is for governments give coupons (vouchers) for switches from [internal combustion engines](https://mechanicalboost.com/internal-combustion-engine/) to [continuous motion servos](https://www.pololu.com/blog/24/continuous-rotation-servos-and-multi-turn-servos) (which have sufficient torque for propulsion if housed with a ["transmission" / reducer](https://advancedtransmission.com/tesla-transmissions-how-they-handle-electric-motor/), such as the [_Tesla Model S_](https://www.edmunds.com/tesla/model-s/) uses).

One of the 2 biggest problems with cars which use servos is: joules = petrol\*pounds = lithium\*pounds\*2 (the pounds of lithium to power the servos are double, versus the pounds of gas for combustion use).
- This problem is reduced due to the lower mass, plus lower cubic volume, of continuous servos.
- Trivia: kilocals/4184 = kilowatthours/3600000 = joules.

Another of the 2 biggests problems is that Earth does not have sufficient accessible lithium to act as an absolute substitute for gas.
- The cost of lithium power sources is the reason that most can not afford the _Tesla Model S_.

- [**Biomimetic robots run off of proton sources (as humans do, as opposes to batteries based off of electrons)**](https://swudususuwu.substack.com/p/biomimetic-robots-run-off-of-proton)

"Hybrid" (dual-power-source) cars (plus pure electrics such as _Teslas_) use callipers+drums due to insufficient dynamo resistances (continuous motion servos used as dynamos can not stop cars within seconds, as callipers+drums can do). The problem is that callipers+drums do not convert motions into charge which the car can use.
- Callipers+drums convert all motion into thermal emissions.

To reduce the amount of power usage (thus reduce the mass + cubic-volume of power sources):
- Can advanced gearboxes/transmissions (with sufficient gear ratios) improve resistance, so that servos as dynamos can stop cars in seconds (with the forward motion converted into charge) so that gas use improves?
- What about servo reducers for [tools such as *Kuka*s / *Fanuc*s](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hLDbRm-98cs); can those convert kinetic force (at the end of repetitious motions) back into power which those tools can reuse, so that grid power use improves?
