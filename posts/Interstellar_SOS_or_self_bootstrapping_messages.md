[**How extraterrestrials could broadcast SOS (or send self-bootstrapping messages)**](https://swudususuwu.substack.com/p/how-extraterrestrials-could-broadcast-sos)

Photons can traverse space, plus encode "Arecibo messages" (such as universal constants or calculus).

Extraterrestrials could broadcast their existences through various mediums such as photons (radio waves, microwaves, xrays, gamma rays, are all photons <https://physicsworld.com/a/single-photon-can-encode-quantum-information-in-10-dimensions/>), or gravitons (particle accelerators consume more power, but gravitons pass through mass: <https://home.cern/news/news/accelerators/accelerators-meet-gravitational-waves>) to broadcast descriptions of calculus, or of universal constants.

__________

To signal distress, extraterrestrials can insert special errors (such that natural interference can not have caused such errors) to those broadcasts.
- For instance, you can broadcast universal constants — or, through [categorical data encoders](https://medium.com/aiskunks/categorical-data-encoding-techniques-d6296697a40f) suchas [_Gödel Numberings_](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C3%B6del_numbering) or [_Friedberg Numberings_](https://www.ams.org/journals/proc/1964-015-02/S0002-9939-1964-0174479-5/S0002-9939-1964-0174479-5.pdf), broadcast calculuses, — with [error-correction codes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Error_correction_code) (so that deliberate (**SOS**) errors are not confused with natural interference), with those **SOS** errors inserted into each ith broadcast (such as 1 of 2 broadcasts have the same errors) to signal distress/**SOS**.
- Without special errors, such messages amount to [self-bootstrapping messages](https://poe.com/s/JEejS4r8Jo2iONf9WX38), similar to [the _Arecibo Message_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communication_with_extraterrestrial_intelligence) or [the _Dumas-Dutil Messages_](https://www.plover.com/misc/Dumas-Dutil/messages.pdf)).

Such an **SOS** can signal that, although the species exists long enough to produce tools so complex it is outrageous, that such species has lost all hope except to [ask foreign organisms to assist](https://www.animalsaroundtheglobe.com/14-instances-of-interspecies-cooperation-in-the-wild-2-321128/).

If problems such as [global warming plus pollution](./Global_warming,_what_to_do.md) do not improve, perhaps humans should broadcast (to close exoplanets) such an **SOS**?
